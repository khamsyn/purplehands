Purple Heart Foundation

For Marginalized Women and Children

We, the members of PURPLEHEART FOUNDATION FOR MARGINALIZED WOMEN AND CHILDREN, a not for-profit and non-political organisation aim to:
promoting the welfare of the less priviledged providing educational support among the nations providing education and support to marginalized children and orphans through community based child care centers.

About Us

PURPLEHEART FOUNDATION is a non governmental organization that has been touching lives of over 500 women and children in several communities within the northern region of Nigeria.
PURPLEHEART simply means STRENGTH to the marginalized women and children.

Health

  • Environmental sanitation awareness
  • House hold hygiene practices
  • Nutrition for pregnant women and under five children.
  • Health service delivery

Education

  • Advocating and support for the girl child education.
  • Contribution to make learning environment in rural communities conducive.

Empowerment

Financial support and training for women and girl child in areas such as:

  • Fashion designing.
  • Catering services.
  • Event planning.
